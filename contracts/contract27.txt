**Data Protection Clause** **1. Definitions** 1.1. "Data Subject" shall mean any identified or identifiable natural person whose Personal Data is processed under this Agreement. 1.2. "Personal Data" shall mean any information relating to a Data Subject, which is collected, used, or processed in connection with the performance of this Agreement. 1.3. "Processing" shall mean any operation or set of operations performed upon Personal Data, whether or not by automated means, including, but not limited to, collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, alignment, combination, restriction, erasure or destruction. 1.4. "Controller" and "Processor" shall have the same meanings as defined in the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable local data protection laws. **2. Data Protection Obligations** 2.1. Compliance with Laws: Both Parties agree to comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the GDPR and relevant domestic legislation, while processing Personal Data under this Agreement. 2.2. Lawful Basis: Each Party shall ensure that there is a lawful basis for the processing of Personal Data as prescribed under applicable data protection laws. 2.3. Data Minimization: The Parties agree to adhere to the principle of data minimization, ensuring that Personal Data processed is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. 2.4. Accuracy: Each Party shall ensure that the Personal Data processed is accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date. Parties shall take every reasonable step to ensure that inaccurate Personal Data is erased or rectified without delay. **3. Security Measures** 3.1. Technical and Organizational Measures: The Parties shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect Personal Data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. 3.2. Access Controls: Personal Data shall be accessed only by personnel who have a legitimate need to access that data for the purposes of this Agreement and who are subject to confidentiality obligations. 3.3. Data Breach Notification: In the event of a Personal Data breach, the responsible Party shall promptly notify the other Party and the relevant supervisory authority without undue delay and in compliance with applicable data protection regulations. **4. Rights of Data Subjects** 4.1. Subject Access Requests: Each Party shall assist the other in responding to any requests from a Data Subject to exercise their rights under applicable data protection laws, including rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability. 4.2. Objection to Processing: If a Data Subject objects to the processing of their Personal Data, the Parties shall cooperate to ensure compliance with applicable legal requirements concerning such objection. **5. Transfer of Personal Data** 5.1. Cross-Border Data Transfers: The Parties shall ensure that any transfer of Personal Data across borders complies with applicable data protection laws. Where required, appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or binding corporate rules must be in place. 5.2. Sub-Processors: If a Party engages sub-processors to process Personal Data, it shall ensure that such sub-processors are bound by obligations equivalent to those contained in this Clause. **6. Data Retention and Deletion** 6.1. Retention Policy: The Parties shall retain Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Agreement, or as required by applicable law. 6.2. Secure Deletion: Upon termination of this Agreement or when Personal Data is no longer required, the responsible Party shall ensure the secure deletion or return of Personal Data, as appropriate. **7. Audit and Compliance** 7.1. Audit Rights: Each Party agrees to provide the other with sufficient information to demonstrate compliance with the obligations set out in this Clause, and to allow for and contribute to audits conducted by the other Party or an auditor mandated by the other Party, subject to reasonable confidentiality obligations. **8. Indemnity and Liability** 8.1. Indemnity: Each Party agrees to indemnify and hold the other Party harmless against all claims, actions, liabilities, losses, damages, and expenses arising out of or in connection with any breach of this Data Protection Clause. 8.2. Limitation of Liability: Except as otherwise provided in this Agreement, neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages arising out of or relating to this Data Protection Clause. **9. Miscellaneous** 9.1. Amendments: This Data Protection Clause may be amended only by a written agreement duly executed by both Parties. 9.2.
