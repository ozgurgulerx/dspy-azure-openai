**Data Protection Clause** **1. Introduction** 1.1 This Data Protection Clause (hereinafter referred to as the “Clause”) is incorporated into the Agreement to set forth the terms, conditions, and obligations of the parties with respect to the handling, processing, protection, and security of Personal Data and Sensitive Data (hereinafter collectively referred to as “Data”) that are collected, accessed, or otherwise processed during the course of the Agreement. **2. Definitions** 2.1 “Data Protection Laws” shall refer to all applicable national, federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and guidelines pertaining to data protection, privacy, and security, including, but not limited to, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and any other analogous legislation. 2.2 “Personal Data” shall mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person as defined under applicable Data Protection Laws. 2.3 “Sensitive Data” shall include, without limitation, data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, genetic data, biometric data for the purpose of uniquely identifying a natural person, and data concerning health or a natural person’s sex life or sexual orientation. **3. Data Processing and Protection** 3.1 The Parties agree to process Data solely for the legitimate and lawful purposes as outlined in this Agreement and in accordance with applicable Data Protection Laws. 3.2 The Parties shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including, as appropriate:    i. The pseudonymization and encryption of Personal Data;    ii. The ability to ensure ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience of processing systems and services;    iii. The ability to restore the availability and access to Personal Data in a timely manner in the event of a physical or technical incident;    iv. A process for regularly testing, assessing, and evaluating the effectiveness of technical and organizational measures for ensuring the security of the processing. **4. Data Subject Rights** 4.1 The Parties shall uphold the rights of Data Subjects in accordance with applicable Data Protection Laws, including, but not limited to:    i. The right to access, rectify, or erase Personal Data;    ii. The right to restrict or object to the processing of Personal Data;    iii. The right to data portability;    iv. The right to withdraw consent where consent is the lawful basis for processing. **5. Data Breach Notification** 5.1 In the event of a Personal Data Breach, the affected Party shall notify the other Party without undue delay and, in any event, within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware of the breach, where feasible. 5.2 The notification shall include:    i. The nature of the Personal Data Breach including, where possible, the categories and approximate number of Data Subjects concerned and the categories and approximate number of data records concerned;    ii. The name and contact details of the data protection officer or other contact point where more information can be obtained;    iii. The likely consequences of the Personal Data Breach;    iv. The measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Party to address the Personal Data Breach, including, where appropriate, measures to mitigate its possible adverse effects. **6. Data Transfers** 6.1 The transfer of Data to a third country or an international organization shall only occur under the condition that the transferring Party implements appropriate safeguards in compliance with Data Protection Laws, ensuring that all rights of Data Subjects are maintained and enforced. 6.2 Any transfer of Personal Data across borders must comply with the provisions of applicable Data Protection Laws regarding international data transfers, including the use of standard contractual clauses or other approved transfer mechanisms. **7. Confidentiality and Access Control** 7.1 The Parties shall ensure that individuals authorized to process the Data have committed themselves to confidentiality or are under an appropriate statutory obligation of confidentiality. 7.2 Access to Data shall be strictly controlled and limited to those individuals who require such access to fulfill the terms and objectives of the Agreement. **8. Data Retention and Destruction** 8.1 Data shall be retained only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes of processing as set out in the Agreement, or as required by applicable laws and regulations. 8.2 Upon expiry of the retention period, the Party processing Data shall securely delete or destroy the Data in a manner that ensures it cannot be reconstructed or read. **9. Compliance and Audit** 9.1 The Parties agree to cooperate with each other in order to demonstrate compliance with the obligations laid down in this Clause, including allowing for and contributing to audits. 9.2 Each Party shall maintain records of processing activities under its responsibility in accordance with applicable Data Protection Laws, and these records shall be made available to the other Party upon request. **10. Indemnity** 10.1 Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Party from and against all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising out of or in connection with any breach of this Clause, including failure to comply with applicable Data Protection Laws. **11. Amendments** 11.1 Any amendment, supplement, or modification of this Clause shall be made in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both Parties, and shall comply with applicable Data Protection Laws. **12. Governing Law** 12.1 This Clause shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws specified in the governing law section of the Agreement.
