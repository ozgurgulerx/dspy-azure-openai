**Data Protection Clause** **1. Introduction** 1.1. This Data Protection Clause (hereinafter referred to as the "Clause") outlines the obligations of the Parties concerning the processing, protection, and security of Personal Data (as defined below) in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679 or "GDPR") and any other relevant national legislation (collectively, the "Data Protection Laws"). **2. Definitions** 2.1. "Data Controller" and "Data Processor" shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the GDPR. 2.2. "Data Subject" refers to an identified or identifiable natural person to whom the Personal Data relates. 2.3. "Personal Data" refers to any information relating to a Data Subject that is protected under Data Protection Laws. 2.4. "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data, whether by automated means or otherwise, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, or otherwise making available, alignment, combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction. 2.5. "Sensitive Data" refers to Personal Data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or trade union membership, genetic data, biometric data for the purpose of uniquely identifying a natural person, data concerning health, or data concerning a natural person's sex life or sexual orientation. **3. Compliance with Data Protection Laws** 3.1. Each Party agrees to comply with all applicable Data Protection Laws in relation to the Processing of Personal Data under this Agreement. 3.2. Each Party shall ensure that its personnel, agents, and subcontractors are informed of and comply with the relevant Data Protection Laws in their Processing activities. **4. Roles and Responsibilities** 4.1. The Parties acknowledge that, with respect to Personal Data, one Party may act as the Data Controller and the other as the Data Processor, or both Parties may act as Joint Data Controllers. The specific roles shall be determined based on the actual Processing activities performed pursuant to this Agreement. 4.2. Where one Party acts as a Data Processor, it shall:    a) Process the Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Data Controller;
   b) Ensure the confidentiality of the Personal Data and protect it against unauthorized or unlawful Processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage, using appropriate technical and organizational measures;
   c) Assist the Data Controller, insofar as possible, in responding to Data Subject requests and ensuring compliance with obligations under Data Protection Laws; **5. Security Measures** 5.1. Each Party agrees to implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including, inter alia, as appropriate:    a) The pseudonymization and encryption of Personal Data;
   b) The ability to ensure the ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience of Processing systems and services;
   c) The ability to restore the availability and access to Personal Data in a timely manner in the event of a physical or technical incident;
   d) A process for regularly testing, assessing, and evaluating the effectiveness of technical and organizational measures for ensuring the security of the Processing. **6. Data Breach Notification** 6.1. In the event of a Personal Data Breach, the Data Processor shall notify the Data Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of the breach. The notification shall include, to the extent possible, details concerning the nature of the breach, the categories and approximate number of Data Subjects affected, and the measures taken or proposed to be taken to address the breach. **7. Data Transfers** 7.1. The Parties shall not transfer Personal Data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) or any other jurisdiction without ensuring appropriate safeguards in accordance with Data Protection Laws, such as entering into Standard Contractual Clauses or relying on other legal bases for the transfer. **8. Data Retention and Deletion** 8.1. The Parties agree to retain Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes of the Processing and shall delete or anonymize the Personal Data upon the termination of this Agreement or completion of the Processing activities, unless a longer retention period is required by law. **9. Indemnification** 9.1. Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Party against any claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising out of or in connection with a breach of this Clause. **10. Governing Law and Jurisdiction** 10.1. This Clause shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [Jurisdiction]. Any disputes arising from or relating to this Clause shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of [Jurisdiction]. **11. Miscellaneous** 11.1. This Clause constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding data protection and supersedes all previous understandings, agreements, or representations regarding the subject matter herein. 11.2.
