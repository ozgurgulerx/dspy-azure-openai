**Data Protection Clause** **1. Introduction** This Data Protection Clause (hereinafter referred to as the "Clause") forms an integral part of the Agreement entered into by and between [Party A], a corporation organized under the laws of [Jurisdiction], with its principal place of business at [Address], and [Party B], a corporation organized under the laws of [Jurisdiction], with its principal place of business at [Address] (collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party"). This Clause is intended to ensure the protection and confidentiality of Personal Data (as defined herein) processed in the course of executing the Agreement. **2. Definitions** 2.1. "Personal Data" refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. 2.2. "Processing" means any operation or set of operations which is performed on Personal Data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction. 2.3. "Data Controller" means the Party which, alone or jointly with others, determines the purposes and means of the processing of Personal Data. 2.4. "Data Processor" means the Party which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller. 2.5. "Applicable Data Protection Laws" refers to all regional, national, and international laws and regulations relating to the Processing of Personal Data that are applicable to the Parties, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), and any data protection laws of [Jurisdiction]. **3. General Obligations** 3.1. Compliance: The Parties agree to comply with their respective obligations under the Applicable Data Protection Laws in relation to the Processing of Personal Data. 3.2. Lawful Processing: Each Party warrants that all Personal Data processed in connection with the Agreement has been and will be collected, processed, and shared in compliance with the Applicable Data Protection Laws. **4. Data Controller and Processor Obligations** 4.1. Data Controller Responsibilities: The Data Controller shall ensure that all necessary legal bases for Processing Personal Data are established, including obtaining consents where necessary, and providing all appropriate notices to data subjects regarding the Processing of their Personal Data. 4.2. Data Processor Responsibilities: The Data Processor agrees to process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Data Controller, unless required to do so by law. **5. Confidentiality and Security Measures** 5.1. Confidentiality: The Parties shall maintain the confidentiality of all Personal Data and shall ensure that any person acting under their authority who has access to Personal Data is obliged to maintain its confidentiality, whether or not such person is a party to this Agreement. 5.2. Security Measures: The Parties shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including, as appropriate, the pseudonymization and encryption of Personal Data, the ability to ensure the ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience of processing systems and services, and the ability to restore the availability and access to Personal Data in a timely manner in the event of a physical or technical incident. **6. Sub-Processing and International Data Transfers** 6.1. Sub-Processing: The Data Processor shall not engage another processor without prior specific or general written authorization from the Data Controller. Where general written authorization is given, the Data Processor shall inform the Data Controller of any intended changes concerning the addition or replacement of other processors, thereby giving the Data Controller the opportunity to object to such changes. 6.2. International Transfers: Personal Data shall not be transferred outside the jurisdiction of origin without ensuring adequate protection in compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws, which may include entering into data transfer agreements that incorporate Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the relevant authorities. **7. Data Subject Rights** 7.1. The Parties shall assist each other in fulfilling their respective legal obligations to honor the rights of Data Subjects, including but not limited to rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection, as stipulated under Applicable Data Protection Laws. **8. Data Breach Notification** 8.1. Each Party shall notify the other without undue delay upon becoming aware of any Personal Data Breach. Such notification shall, where feasible, include details of the nature of the breach, the categories and approximate number of data subjects likely to be affected, and the measures taken or proposed to address the breach. **9. Retention and Disposal** 9.1. The Parties shall retain Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and processed or as required by applicable law, regulation, or contractual obligation. 9.2. Upon termination of the Agreement or at the Data Controller's request, the Data Processor shall, at the Data Controller's discretion, return or securely dispose of all Personal Data, unless further storage is required by applicable law. **10. Indemnity** 10.1. Each Party shall indemnify and hold the other Party harmless from any cost, charge, damages, expense, or loss which is caused by its breach of this Clause or Applicable Data Protection Laws. **11. Amendment and Review** 11.1. The Parties agree to review and, where necessary, amend this Clause to ensure compliance with changes in law and regulations or any significant changes in the nature of the Processing.
