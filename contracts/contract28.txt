**Confidentiality Agreement** **1. Definitions** 1.1 "Confidential Information" shall mean any data or information that is proprietary to the Disclosing Party and not generally known to the public, whether in tangible or intangible form, whenever and however disclosed, including, but not limited to, any financial information, business strategies, customer lists, trade secrets, technical data, formulas, algorithms, research, software, and any other scientific or business information. 1.2 "Disclosing Party" refers to the party disclosing Confidential Information to the Receiving Party. 1.3 "Receiving Party" refers to the party receiving Confidential Information from the Disclosing Party. **2. Obligations of Confidentiality** 2.1 The Receiving Party hereby acknowledges and agrees: 2.1.1 To maintain in strict confidence any and all Confidential Information provided by the Disclosing Party. 2.1.2 Not to disclose, disseminate, or communicate Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party. 2.1.3 To use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose for which it was disclosed and for no other purpose whatsoever, without the express prior written consent of the Disclosing Party. 2.1.4 To implement reasonable security precautions to protect the confidentiality of the Confidential Information, at least as stringent as those it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no case less than a reasonable standard of care. 2.1.5 To ensure that access to the Confidential Information is restricted to the Receiving Party's employees, agents, or representatives who have a legitimate need to know such information and who are bound to confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those set forth herein. **3. Exclusions** 3.1 The obligations of confidentiality under this agreement shall not extend to information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate: 3.1.1 Was in the public domain at the time of disclosure or has entered the public domain through no fault or breach of this agreement by the Receiving Party. 3.1.2 Was lawfully in the possession of the Receiving Party prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party, as evidenced by written documentation. 3.1.3 Was obtained from a third party without breach of any obligation of confidentiality owed to the Disclosing Party. 3.1.4 Was independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to or reliance upon the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information, as established by written records. **4. Mandatory Disclosure** 4.1 Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Receiving Party may disclose the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information to the extent required by law, regulation, or court order, provided that the Receiving Party: 4.1.1 Provides prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party of the requirement to disclose, where legally permissible, allowing the Disclosing Party a reasonable opportunity to object or seek a protective order. 4.1.2 Discloses only such portion of the Confidential Information as is legally required and uses commercially reasonable efforts to obtain assurance that the Confidential Information will be treated confidentially by the party to whom it is disclosed. **5. Return or Destruction of Confidential Information** 5.1 Upon termination of this agreement or upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or, at the Receiving Party’s option, destroy all copies of the Confidential Information in its possession or control, including any materials derived therefrom, and certify in writing to the Disclosing Party that it has complied with such obligation. **6. Term** 6.1 The term of confidentiality obligations under this agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of five (5) years following the termination of this agreement, except with respect to Trade Secrets, for which the obligations shall continue indefinitely as long as such information remains a Trade Secret under applicable law. **7. Remedies** 7.1 The Receiving Party acknowledges that any breach of its obligations under this agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Disclosing Party for which monetary damages may be difficult to ascertain or an inadequate remedy. Therefore, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunctive relief, specific performance, or any other relief as may be available from any court of competent jurisdiction, without the necessity of posting a bond or other security or proving actual damages. **8. Miscellaneous** 8.1 **Governing Law:** This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Disclosing Party principally conducts business, without regard to its conflicts of law principles. 8.2 **Severability:** If any provision of this agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions of this agreement shall continue in full force and effect. 8.3 **Entire Agreement:** This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether oral or written, relating thereto. No amendment or modification of this agreement shall be binding unless executed in writing by both parties. 8.4 **Waiver:** No failure or delay by either party in exercising any right, power, or privilege under this agreement shall operate as a waiver thereof, nor shall any single or partial exercise thereof preclude any other or further exercise of any right, power, or privilege hereunder.
