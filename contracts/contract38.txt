### Confidentiality Agreement #### Section 1: Definitions 1.1 "Confidential Information" shall mean any data or information, oral or written, disclosed by one Party ("Disclosing Party") to the other Party ("Receiving Party"), that is not generally known to the public and is provided under this Agreement, including but not limited to, business plans, technical data, trade secrets, customer lists, financial information, marketing strategies, product designs, software, and any other proprietary information that is designated as confidential or that, under the circumstances surrounding disclosure, ought to be treated as confidential.  1.2 "Representatives" shall mean directors, officers, employees, agents, consultants, advisors, or any other authorized individuals who are duly bound by obligations of confidentiality no less restrictive than those contained herein. #### Section 2: Obligations of Confidentiality 2.1 The Receiving Party hereby agrees to hold and maintain the Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party in strictest confidence for the sole and exclusive benefit of the Disclosing Party. 2.2 The Receiving Party shall not, without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, disclose, publish, or make available any Confidential Information furnished by the Disclosing Party to any third party, except to its Representatives, and only to the extent necessary to fulfill the intent and purpose of the parties under this Agreement. 2.3 The Receiving Party agrees to take all reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality of and avoid disclosure or unauthorized use of the Confidential Information. Such measures shall include implementing protection protocols that are at least as stringent as those it uses with its own Confidential Information. #### Section 3: Permitted Disclosures 3.1 The confidentiality obligations stated herein shall not be applicable to any portion of Confidential Information for which the Receiving Party can demonstrate with appropriate documentary evidence that:    (a) It is or has become publicly known through no wrongful act of the Receiving Party or its Representatives;
   
   (b) It was known by the Receiving Party prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party;    (c) It is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to or use of the Confidential Information;    (d) It is required to be disclosed pursuant to a binding court order, governmental regulation, or statute, provided that the Receiving Party promptly notifies the Disclosing Party of such legal obligation and cooperates with any efforts of the Disclosing Party to contest or limit the scope of the required disclosure. #### Section 4: Return of Materials 4.1 Upon the termination of this Agreement, or upon the written request of the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or, at the Disclosing Party's instruction, destroy all documents and other tangible materials representing the Confidential Information and all copies thereof. 4.2 The Receiving Party shall provide written certification of such return or destruction within thirty (30) days upon request by the Disclosing Party. #### Section 5: Intellectual Property Rights 5.1 The Receiving Party acknowledges that the Confidential Information and any derivations thereof are the sole and exclusive property of the Disclosing Party and that this Agreement grants no license, interest, or rights in these intellectual properties, except as strictly specified under this Agreement. 5.2 No express or implied license or right is granted to the Receiving Party under any patents, copyrights, trademarks, or trade secret information through disclosure made pursuant to this Agreement. #### Section 6: Duration 6.1 The obligations to maintain the confidentiality outlined in this Agreement shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years from the date of such termination or expiration. #### Section 7: Remedies 7.1 The Receiving Party agrees that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Disclosing Party for which monetary damages would be inadequate and that, in addition to any other remedies at law or in equity, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief to enforce the terms of this Agreement without the necessity of proving actual damages. 7.2 The Receiving Party agrees to indemnify and hold the Disclosing Party harmless from any damage, loss, or expense incurred due to any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information by the Receiving Party or its Representatives. #### Section 8: Governing Law 8.1 The construction, interpretation, and performance of this Confidentiality Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [Insert State], without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any legal action or proceeding arising under this Agreement shall be brought exclusively in the competent courts located within that State. #### Section 9: Miscellaneous 9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior discussions, agreements, and understandings of any kind between the Parties. 9.2 If any provision of this Agreement is adjudicated to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the validity, legality, and enforceability of the remaining provisions shall not in any way be affected or impaired thereby. 9.3 This Agreement may not be amended or terminated except by a written agreement signed by both Parties.
