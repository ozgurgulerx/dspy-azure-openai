**Data Protection Clause** 1. **Definitions**  
   For the purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed to them herein:
   
   1.1 "Data Protection Laws" shall mean all applicable laws, regulations, and directives relating to the processing of personal data and privacy, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) ("GDPR"), the Data Protection Act 2018, and any subsequent amendments, re-enactments, or supersessions thereof.    1.2 "Personal Data" shall refer to any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person as defined by the applicable Data Protection Laws.    1.3 "Processing" shall have the meaning set forth in the applicable Data Protection Laws and shall include any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data, whether or not by automated means.
   
   1.4 "Data Controller" and "Data Processor" shall have the meanings given in the applicable Data Protection Laws. 2. **Data Controller Obligations**    2.1 The Parties acknowledge that one Party may operate as a Data Controller for the purpose of Processing Personal Data under this Agreement. The Data Controller shall ensure compliance with all relevant provisions of the Data Protection Laws in its handling of Personal Data.    2.2 The Data Controller shall provide clear and explicit information to Data Subjects regarding the Processing of their Personal Data, including but not limited to the purposes for Processing, the legal basis for Processing, and the rights available to Data Subjects under the Data Protection Laws. 3. **Data Processor Obligations**    3.1 Where one Party acts as a Data Processor on behalf of the Data Controller, the Data Processor agrees to Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Data Controller, except where required by applicable law.    3.2 The Data Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, in accordance with Article 32 of the GDPR.    3.3 The Data Processor shall ensure that any personnel authorized to Process Personal Data are subject to confidentiality obligations. 4. **Sub-processing**    4.1 The Data Processor shall not engage any sub-processor without prior specific or general written authorization from the Data Controller.    4.2 When authorized to engage a sub-processor, the Data Processor shall ensure that such sub-processor agrees to comply with equivalent data protection obligations as set out in this Clause. The Data Processor shall remain liable for the sub-processor’s actions. 5. **Data Subject Rights**    5.1 The Parties shall assist each other where necessary, in responding to requests from Data Subjects exercising their rights under Data Protection Laws, including but not limited to rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of Processing, data portability, and objection. 6. **Data Breach Notification**    6.1 In the event of a Personal Data Breach, the Data Processor shall notify the Data Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of the breach.    6.2 The notification shall at a minimum:    (a) Describe the nature of the Personal Data Breach including, where possible, the categories and approximate number of Data Subjects concerned;
   
   (b) Communicate the likely consequences of the Personal Data Breach;    (c) Describe the measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Data Processor to address the Personal Data Breach, including, where appropriate, measures to mitigate its possible adverse effects. 7. **International Data Transfers**    7.1 The Data Processor shall not transfer Personal Data to a third country or international organization without obtaining explicit written instructions from the Data Controller and ensuring that such transfer complies with the requirements of the Data Protection Laws. 8. **Data Retention and Deletion**    8.1 The Data Processor shall retain Personal Data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Agreement or as required by applicable law.    8.2 Upon termination of this Agreement, and at the direction of the Data Controller, the Data Processor shall either securely delete or return all Personal Data to the Data Controller, and delete existing copies unless applicable law requires further retention. 9. **Audit and Compliance**    9.1 The Data Processor shall make available to the Data Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with the obligations set forth in this Data Protection Clause and allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by the Data Controller or another auditor mandated by the Data Controller. 10. **Liability and Indemnity**    10.1 Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Party against any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising out of or in connection with any breach by that Party of its obligations under this Data Protection Clause. 11. **Miscellaneous**    11.1 Should any provision of this Data Protection Clause be found to be invalid or unenforceable, that provision shall be deemed modified to the extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable, or severed from this Clause, and shall not affect the remainder of this Clause.    11.2 The obligations set forth in this Data Protection Clause shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement for any reason.
