**Section X: Data Protection Clause** **1. Introduction and Scope** 1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that they shall at all times comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), and any other applicable privacy laws that may apply in the jurisdiction(s) in which the Parties operate. 1.2 This Data Protection Clause ("Clause") serves to establish the rights and obligations of the Parties concerning the collection, processing, storage, and protection of Personal Data and Sensitive Data exchanged under this Agreement. **2. Definitions** 2.1 "Personal Data" shall mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ("Data Subject"). 2.2 "Sensitive Data" shall refer to Personal Data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, genetic data, biometric data for the purpose of uniquely identifying a natural person, data concerning health, or data concerning a natural person's sex life or sexual orientation. 2.3 "Processing" shall have the meaning ascribed to it under the GDPR, including but not limited to the collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction of data. **3. Data Collection and Processing** 3.1 The Parties shall only collect and process Personal Data and Sensitive Data that is necessary for the performance of the contractual obligations under this Agreement. 3.2 Each Party shall ensure that Personal Data is collected lawfully, fairly, and transparently, in accordance with the privacy rights and interests of Data Subjects. 3.3 The Parties shall implement appropriate legal bases for Processing, such as obtaining explicit consent from the Data Subjects or ensuring that Processing is necessary for the performance of this Agreement or compliance with legal obligations. **4. Data Security** 4.1. The Parties shall take all appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of Personal Data and Sensitive Data, protecting it against unauthorized or unlawful Processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage. 4.2 Such measures shall include, but are not limited to, encryption, pseudonymization, access controls, regular security audits, and the implementation of security protocols and policies. **5. Data Transfer and Sharing** 5.1. The Parties shall not transfer or share Personal Data or Sensitive Data with any third party without ensuring adequate protection measures consistent with the applicable data protection regulations. 5.2. Cross-border transfers of Personal Data shall comply with the specific legal requirements, including but not limited to executing Standard Contractual Clauses or relying on other mechanisms approved by the applicable data protection authorities. **6. Data Subject Rights** 6.1. Each Party agrees to uphold the rights of Data Subjects and to facilitate the exercise of those rights, which may include the right of access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction of Processing, data portability, and the right to object to Processing. 6.2. The Parties shall implement procedures to fulfill these rights, and any requests from Data Subjects shall be addressed in a timely and efficient manner. **7. Breach Notification** 7.1. In the event of a data breach affecting Personal Data or Sensitive Data, the affected Party shall without undue delay, and where feasible, within 72 hours after having become aware of it, notify the other Party and any relevant data protection authorities. 7.2. The notification shall include, at a minimum, the nature of the data breach, the categories and approximate number of Data Subjects concerned, and the measures taken to mitigate any potential adverse effects. **8. Data Retention and Deletion** 8.1. The Parties shall retain Personal Data and Sensitive Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes identified in this Agreement, unless a longer retention period is required by law. 8.2. Upon termination of this Agreement, or upon earlier request by either Party, all Personal Data and Sensitive Data shall be returned or securely deleted in accordance with the applicable data protection laws. **9. Indemnity** 9.1. Each Party agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other Party against any and all claims, demands, liabilities, damages, losses, costs, and expenses, including reasonable legal fees, arising out of or in connection with any breach by the indemnifying Party of its obligations under this Clause. **10. Miscellaneous** 10.1. This Clause constitutes the entire understanding and agreement of the Parties with respect to the protection of Personal Data and supersedes all prior discussions, agreements, or understandings of any kind. 10.2.
