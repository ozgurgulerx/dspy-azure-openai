**Data Protection Clause** **1. Introduction and Objectives** 1.1 The purpose of this Data Protection Clause is to ensure that the parties comply with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and corresponding domestic data protection legislation. The parties acknowledge the importance of ensuring privacy and protection of personal data. 1.2 The objectives of this Clause are to delineate responsibilities, define the terms of data processing, and safeguard the rights of individuals whose personal data is collected, processed, or transferred pursuant to this Agreement. **2. Definitions** 2.1 "Personal Data" shall mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ('Data Subject'). 2.2 "Processing" shall mean any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, or otherwise making available, alignment, combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction. 2.3 "Data Controller" and "Data Processor" shall be defined as per their meanings in the GDPR. **3. Data Controller and Data Processor Roles** 3.1 The parties agree to determine their respective roles as Data Controller or Data Processor. Each party shall perform its duties in accordance with applicable data protection laws. 3.2 The Data Controller shall ensure that all Personal Data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently, and shall be responsible for informing the Data Subjects about the data processing and their rights. **4. Processing of Personal Data** 4.1 The Data Processor undertakes to process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Data Controller unless required to do so by law. 4.2 The Data Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data have committed themselves to confidentiality or are under an appropriate statutory obligation of confidentiality. 4.3 The processing of Personal Data by the Data Processor shall be reasonable, necessary, and proportionate to the purposes of fulfilling obligations under this Agreement. **5. Security Measures** 5.1 The Data Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including but not limited to pseudonymization and encryption of Personal Data, the ability to ensure ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience of processing systems and services. 5.2 The Data Processor shall regularly test, assess, and evaluate the effectiveness of technical and organizational measures for ensuring the security of processing. **6. Sub-processors** 6.1 The Data Processor shall not engage another processor without the prior specific or general written authorization of the Data Controller. 6.2 Where the Data Processor engages a sub-processor for carrying out specific processing activities, the same data protection obligations as set out in this Clause shall be imposed on that sub-processor by way of a binding contract. **7. Data Subject Rights** 7.1 The Data Processor shall assist the Data Controller by appropriate technical and organizational measures, insofar as this is possible, for the fulfillment of the Data Controller's obligation to respond to requests for exercising the Data Subject's rights. 7.2 The Data Controller reserves the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, or port Personal Data, and the Data Processor shall facilitate such rights in cooperation with the Data Controller. **8. Data Breach Notification** 8.1 The Data Processor shall, without undue delay, notify the Data Controller upon becoming aware of a personal data breach, and provide the Data Controller with a description of the nature of the breach, the likely consequences, and proposed mitigation measures. 8.2 The Data Processor shall not disclose Personal Data Breach notifications to any third party or the public without the prior consent of the Data Controller, except where required by law. **9. Data Transfer** 9.1 The Data Processor shall not transfer Personal Data to a third country or international organization without the Data Controller’s documented instructions, unless required by law, in which case the Data Processor shall inform the Data Controller before such transfer. 9.2 The Data Processor shall ensure that any transfer complies with the conditions for transfer set out in Chapter V of the GDPR. **10. Term and Termination** 10.1 This Clause shall remain in effect as long as the Data Processor processes Personal Data pursuant to this Agreement. 10.2 Upon termination of this Agreement, the Data Processor shall, at the choice of the Data Controller, delete or return all Personal Data to the Data Controller, and delete existing copies unless law requires storage of the Personal Data. **11. Audit Rights** 11.1 The Data Controller reserves the right to audit the Data Processor’s compliance with this Clause, with reasonable prior notice, and the Data Processor shall provide all necessary assistance to facilitate such audits. **12. Liability and Indemnification** 12.1 Each party shall be liable for any damages arising from a breach of its obligations under this Data Protection Clause and agrees to indemnify the other party against any fines or penalties imposed by a data protection authority due to non-compliance attributable to that party. **13. Miscellaneous** 13.1 In the event of any inconsistencies between the provisions of this Data Protection Clause and other agreements between the parties, the provisions of this Clause shall prevail with respect to data protection.
