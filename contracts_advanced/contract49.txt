 **Indemnity Clause Version 1:**

The Indemnifying Party (hereinafter referred to as "Indemnitor") agrees to shield, hold harmless, and compensate the Indemnified Party (hereafter referred to as "Indemnitee") from any and all claims, liabilities, damages, losses, costs, and expenses, including, without limitation, attorney's fees and court costs, arising out of or in connection with any negligent act, omission, or breach of any representation or warranty by the Indemnitor, its agents, or employees, provided such liabilities are not attributable to the gross negligence or willful misconduct of Indemnitee. 

**Indemnity Clause Version 2:**

To the fullest extent permissible by law, [Party A] shall indemnify, defend, and hold [Party B], its affiliates, and their respective officers, directors, and employees, harmless from and against any and all demands, claims, suits, or actions, and any and all liabilities, judgments, losses, expenses, or damages arising therefrom. Such indemnification shall apply to any incident arising from [Party A]'s conduct, negligence, error, or omission, except where said liabilities are solely attributable to [Party B]'s intentional wrongdoing.

**Indemnity Clause Version 3:**

In the event of any third-party claim or action instituted against [Party B], [Party A] shall, upon written notification, assume responsibility for indemnifying and safeguarding [Party B] against all detriments and fiscal encumbrances, including but not limited to reasonable attorneys' fees and associated defense costs. This obligation shall not extend to instances where the cause of action is primarily grounded in the outright negligence or deliberate misconduct of [Party B].

**Indemnity Clause Version 4:**

[Indemnifying Party] shall bear the full burden of indemnification for [Indemnified Party] against any claims, liabilities, damages, or suits (including but not limited to any incurred legal fees and liabilities). This obligation encompasses any defects, negligence, or breaches of obligation stemming from [Indemnifying Party]'s operations, except where such are a direct outcome of [Indemnified Party]'s gross negligence or intentional malfeasance. **Clause A:**

**Force Majeure**: Neither party shall be liable for any delay in performance or failure to perform any obligation under this Agreement caused by unforeseeable circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including, but not limited to, acts of God, war, terrorism, governmental restrictions, natural disasters, or pandemics. Should such an event occur, the obligated party shall notify the other party promptly, and the time for performance shall be extended for the duration of the event.

**Clause B:**

**Events Beyond Control**: In the event that either Party is hindered, prevented, or delayed in performing any of its obligations by reason of circumstances that are beyond the reasonable control of the Party affected, including but not limited to inclement weather, civil commotion, strikes, lockouts, acts of governmental authorities, or interruptions to utilities, such Party shall be excused from such performance to the extent of and for the duration of such impediment. The Party so affected shall provide notice to the other as soon as practicable, resuming performance thereafter.

**Clause C:**

**Acts of Fate**: Should any Party be unable to perform its obligations under this contract due to extraordinary occurrences such as hurricanes, earthquakes, epidemics, labor disputes, or other events that could not reasonably have been anticipated or controlled, the said obligations shall be suspended without liability until such time as performance can reasonably resume. It is incumbent upon the affected Party to exert reasonable effort to overcome the impediments and to communicate the impediments to its counterpart expeditiously.

**Clause D:**

**Extraordinary Circumstances**: If performance by either Party is rendered impossible, delayed, or obstructed by a supervening cause beyond its control, including environmental catastrophes, national emergencies, wartime activities, or any unforeseen interruptions, such Party shall not be deemed in default of this Agreement. Performance shall be postponed for a period equivalent to the delay caused. The burdened Party shall, however, use all diligent efforts to mitigate the impacts and notify the other Party of the occurrence and anticipated duration of the interference.

**Clause E:**

**Unforeseen Impediments**: This Agreement shall be subject to relief from liability where failure or delay in fulfilling any term hereof is due to disruptions such as floods, revolts, cyber-attacks, embargoes, or other incidents beyond the purview and management of the involved Parties. Upon cessation of such conditions, the affected Party shall endeavor to resume obligations promptly. 4. **Force Majeure Clause**  
Neither Party shall be liable for any failure or delay in the performance of its obligations hereunder, if such failure or delay is caused by events beyond reasonable control, including, without limitation, acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemics, strikes, fire, floods, or governmental restrictions. The affected Party shall notify the other Party as soon as practicable and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effect and delay of any such Force Majeure event.

5. **Waiver Clause**  
The failure of either Party to exercise any right, power, or privilege under this Agreement or to insist upon strict compliance with any obligation or provision hereunder, shall not constitute a waiver of any subsequent breach or default, nor a waiver of the Party's right to insist upon strict performance thereafter. Any waiver, amendment, or modification of this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both Parties to be effective.

6. **Assignment and Delegation**  
Neither Party may assign or delegate its rights or duties under this Agreement, in whole or in part, without the prior written consent of the other Party, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, delayed, or conditioned. Any attempt to assign or delegate in contravention of this provision shall be null and void and without effect.

7. **Notices**  
All notifications under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed duly given if delivered by hand, mailed via certified or registered mail, return receipt requested, or sent by recognized overnight courier service, to the respective Parties at their addresses set forth herein or to such other address as a Party may designate in writing, and notices shall be effective upon receipt.

8. **Counterparts Clause**  
This Agreement may be executed in one or more counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which collectively shall constitute one and the same instrument. Delivery of an executed counterpart by facsimile or other electronic means shall be as effective as delivery of a manually executed counterpart.
