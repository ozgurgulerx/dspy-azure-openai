### Non-Disclosure Obligation Clause

#### Version A

The party hereto, herein referred to as the "Receiving Party," hereby covenants and assures the "Disclosing Party" that any and all proprietary insights, knowledge, or revelations disclosed, whether orally, in writing, or through any other medium, shall remain strictly confidential and safeguarded against unwarranted dispersion. The Receiving Party is thereby restricted from divulging, disseminating, or granting access to said insights to third parties, save for those affiliates who have a legitimate necessity to know and are bound by similar privacy pledges.

#### Version B

The undersigned entity, hereinafter acknowledged as the "Receiving Entity," solemnly pledges to uphold the privacy and secrecy of any privileged material encountered from the "Disclosing Entity." This undertaking includes a promise not to broadcast, share, or otherwise make said material available to any external entity without explicit consent, barring exceptions where disclosure is imperative for operational efficacy, as determined by mutual consent.

#### Version C

In recognition of the sensitive essence of the materials exchanged, the "Recipient" hereby commits to preserving the utmost confidentiality of any secretive knowledge, innovative ideas, or proprietary data shared by the "Provider." This encompasses an agreement to refrain from unauthorized sharing or revelation of such materials, except where disclosure is necessary and permissible under prior agreed terms, ensuring such third parties are similarly committed to privacy.

#### Version D

The entity receiving disclosed materials, referred to henceforth as the "Acquiring Party," agrees unequivocally to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the materials received from the "Source.". The Acquiring Party is prohibited from leaking, disclosing, or otherwise propagating the confidential knowledge, save in circumstances requiring dissemination to parties who have agreed to honor the confidentiality of the information with equivalent terms.

#### Version E

The contracted party, from this point on called the "Information Custodian," shall diligently maintain the secrecy of any non-public information received from the "Information Provider." No party shall be held liable for any failure to perform its obligations under this Agreement if such failure is due to circumstances beyond reasonable control, including, but not limited to, acts of God, extreme weather conditions, earthquakes, hurricanes, or any other calamities deemed unusual in nature or severity. In such situations, duties hereunder shall be postponed during the continuation of said unanticipated events. Each party shall use its best efforts to resume performance with all reasonable dispatch after the cessation of the exigent circumstances.

2. **Acts Beyond Control Provision**

In the event that either party is hindered or prevented from executing its part of this contract by unpredictable happenings such as floods, fires, storms, or other acts of nature, neither party shall owe the other for delays or non-performance. These impediments shall be considered outside the scope of the contracting parties' influence. The affected party must notify their counterpart at the earliest opportunity, and efforts to mitigate the impact should be undertaken in good faith.

3. **Uncontrollable Occurrences Release**

The obligations of this contract shall be suspended if a party's ability to perform is materially affected by unexpected phenomena, including environmental disasters like tornadoes, tsunamis, or other large-scale emergencies. Such events must be outside the realm of ordinary foresight and control. Should these extraordinary conditions arise, the parties are encouraged to communicate promptly and explore feasible interim solutions until normalcy resumes.

4. **Exemption from Impossibilities Clause**

This contract acknowledges that certain uncommon events, namely those of a catastrophic nature, may impede or delay fulfillment of contractual commitments. Circumstances such as severe natural occurrences, including but not limited to volcano eruptions or widespread pandemics, shall excuse non-performance. Notification of such impediments should be made with reasonable immediacy, and measures to circumvent the challenges should be implemented wherever possible.
