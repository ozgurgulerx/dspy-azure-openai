Clause 1:  
**Force Majeure Event**  
Neither Party shall be considered in default, or liable for any failure or delay in performance, arising from any unforeseen circumstances or causes beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, war, terrorism, civil unrest, pandemics, governmental restrictions, strikes, lockouts, or other labor disturbances. In such events, the affected Party shall, as soon as practicable, notify the other Party of the occurrence and expected duration of the Force Majeure Event and shall use its reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects of such delay.

Clause 2:  
**Unforeseen Hindrances**  
In the event that either Party's obligations under this Agreement are hindered or prevented due to circumstances that cannot be reasonably controlled, such as natural disasters, industrial actions, health emergencies, or governmental mandates, such Party shall be excused from performance, provided they promptly notify the other Party. The Party affected by such an unforeseen hindrance shall endeavor, with all due diligence, to recommence performance as soon as is practicable.

Clause 3:  
**Acts Beyond Control**  
This Agreement shall be suspended in the event of Force Majeure, wherein 'Force Majeure' shall mean any act, event, or circumstance beyond the reasonable control of the Parties, which includes, but is not limited to, acts of nature, regulations or prohibitions by any government authority, epidemics, or labor disputes. The suspension shall persist only as long as the Force Majeure continues, and the Parties must exert reasonable effort to mitigate and overcome the effects thereof with expediency.

Clause 4:  
**Force Majeure and Non-Liability**  
Neither Party shall bear responsibility for any failure or delay in fulfilling any term or condition of this Agreement due to events beyond their reasonable foresight or control. These may include, but are not limited to, natural disasters, strikes, embargoes, or governmental impositions. Adjustments to deadlines or obligations shall be made upon mutual agreement, and the affected Party shall exercise best efforts to mitigate the impact of such an event.

Clause 5:  
**Irresistible Force**  
The Parties hereto agree that neither will be held accountable for any delay in performance or non-compliance with the terms of this Agreement due to irresistible forces such as natural catastrophes, global health crises, political upheaval, or regulatory embargoes. The Party impeded by such force shall swiftly communicate the situation to the other Party, outlining the nature and projected resolution timeline, and shall resume normal performance duties at the earliest viable opportunity. **Arbitration Clause A**

In recognition of the imperative to protect personal data, each Party commits to managing all information in strict accordance with prevailing privacy laws. Should any Party collect or process personal data, it must implement high-security protocols to ensure confidentiality and integrity. Notification of any unauthorized access or other security incidents that might compromise data must occur immediately upon detection. Optional Phrase: The Parties may also, upon mutual agreement, conduct regular audits to verify compliance.

**3. Personal Information Handling and Security Commitment:**  
Each Party is committed to upholding privacy rights by ensuring the lawful processing of any and all personal data in its possession. This includes, but is not limited to, the adoption of advanced encryption technologies and access controls to prevent unauthorized data disclosures. In instances of data exposure, the responsible Party will promptly report such incidents and collaborate with the impacted Party to mitigate any resulting harm. Optional: The Parties agree to partake in joint training sessions for staff on data protection best practices every six months.

**4. Confidential Data Retention and Compliance Clause:**  
The Parties agree to act diligently in the processing of personal data and to ensure compliance with existing data protection regulations. This encompasses the obligation to limit data access strictly to authorized personnel and to keep data retention to a minimum, in accordance with the principle of data minimization. Breach incidents must be communicated promptly to allow for timely intervention. Optional Phrase: A comprehensive record of processing activities shall be maintained and made available for inspection upon reasonable request.

**5. Data Integrity and Confidentiality Assurance:**  
Each Party shall ensure that the personal data it handles is processed with the utmost care and respect for privacy.
